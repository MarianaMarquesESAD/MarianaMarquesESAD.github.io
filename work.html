A UX (User Experience) designer can offer a variety of services aimed at improving the user experience with digital products such as websites, applications and software. Here are some of the main services I can provide:

  1. User Research
    • Interviews with users
    • Usability tests
    • Competitor analysis
  2. Data analysis
    • Analysis of usage metrics
    • Creation of personas based on data
  3. Information Architecture
    • Creation of sitemaps
    • Structuring content
  4. User Interface Design
    • Wireframe design
    • Design of high and low fidelity prototypes
    • Visual interface design
    • Development of style guides and design systems
  5. Prototyping and testing
    • Creation of interactive prototypes
    • Usability testing
    • Iteration based on user feedback
  6. Accessibility
    • Accessibility audits
    • Design for accessibility
    • Accessibility tests with real users
  7. Multidisciplinary collaboration
    • Working together with developers, product managers and other stakeholders
    • Facilitating brainstorming sessions and design sprints

These services I provide are essential to ensure that digital products are intuitive, efficient and enjoyable for users, resulting in better user satisfaction and greater product success in the market.
